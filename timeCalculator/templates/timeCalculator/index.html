How does it work??

           To demonstrate, use as an example a distance between the earth and a sun which is approximately 149.597.871.000m, the speed of light in the vacuum is a known constant which is approximately 299.792.458m/s. Having this information we just apply the formula and we will have the result in seconds.

Δt = ΔxVm= 149.597.871.000/299.792.458 = 499,00478s

Warning!! It is important to remember that if you want to have the result in minutes or hours it is necessary to make a conversation taking care of the bases because our system of measurement of time works with base 60 and the calculations are in base 10, that is to say ...

Δt=499=480+19=8min 19s
